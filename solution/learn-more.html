The Solution

CDD Bot

Provides Unfettered Internet
IP connectivity is one of the biggest obstacles that organizations face in long-term recovery operations.

Providing unfettered Internet access in an emergency situation is an obvious, logical step.

CDD Bot patrols the area during a disaster, providing internet access to people allowing them to send SOS Messages and provides up-to-the-minute news information. - road closure updates, evacuation routes, designated help areas, shelter locations, and more -

CDD Bot gives people the ability to communicate. Through the internet provided by the CDD Bot Emergency responders can find trapped occupants.

In disaster situations like floods, hurricanes and wildfires, Internet connectivity is as precious as clean water.

Tips for Emergency Communication

Keep all phone calls brief. If you need to use a phone, try to use it only to convey vital information to emergency personnel and/or family.

For non-emergency communications, use text messaging, e-mail, or social media instead of making voice calls on your cell phone to avoid tying up voice networks. Data-based services like texts and emails are less likely to experience network congestion.
